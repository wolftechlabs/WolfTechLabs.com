// SYSTEM_02: MULTI-MATERIAL & ADVANCED 3D PRINTING

Snapmaker U1 (Toolchanger Core)

4 independent physical toolheads optimized for multi-material scaling and custom hardware inserts:

  • Embedded Magnets & Hardware: Non-magnetic brass nozzles allow us to pause prints and seal rare-earth magnets inside structures safely with zero pullout risk mid-print.
  • Dissimilar Material Pairing: Fuses rigid PETG frameworks to rubberized TPU grips or water-soluble PVA support lattices for scar-free mechanical surfaces.

Prusa Mark 4S (Abrasive Core)

Dedicated exclusively to extreme single-material tolerances and destructive composite filaments:

  • Armored Hardened Nozzle: Formulated to run aggressive materials like Glow-in-the-Dark polymers, Carbon Fiber fills, and metal-infused composites without nozzle erosion.
  • Mechanical Synchronization: High-precision manufacturing for interlocking components, gears, and structural casings.
