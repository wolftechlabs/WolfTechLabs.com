The Equipment Fleet & Full Functionalities

Snapmaker U1 Industrial Tool-Changer

Industrial multi-head system using separate dedicated extruders to eliminate material cross-contamination. Configured with a non-magnetic tool head and specialized brass nozzles to fully enable the precision encapsulation of integrated magnets directly inside functional products without hardware interference. Fuses rigid structural polymers (PETG, ABS, ASA) directly with flexible elastomers (TPU) and water-soluble support lines (PVA/BVOH) for complex internal threads and heavy workshop organizers.

Prusa MK4S (MMU3 Integrated)

High-speed, multi-filament precision printing setup optimized for abrasive and advanced materials. Outfitted with a premium hardened steel nozzle setup to cleanly handle high-wear technical lines like carbon fiber composites, metallic infills, and glow-in-the-dark filaments for striking, durable geometric desk sculptures and tight-tolerance custom tabletop gaming miniatures.

xTool M2 20W Multi-Mode Laser Ecosystem

Advanced fabrication workstation merging a high-power 20W diode laser head, a horizontal cylindrical rotary axis, and an integrated inline full-color CMYK print setup. Automatically switches between full-color high-gloss printing and camera-registered laser paths. Delivers rapid 20W raw cutting speeds alongside fine high-contrast surface marking.

Cricut Precision Cutting Systems

High-precision mechanical blade axis optimized for custom sign masking, targeted apparel vinyl stencils, tailored event card elements, and fine-line layout templates across paper, thin plastics, and adhesive media lines.
